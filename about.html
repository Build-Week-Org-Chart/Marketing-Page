About Us

Life is too short to be alone, too precious. Share it with a friend. You have to make these big decisions. You can't make a mistake. Anything that happens you can learn to use - and make something beautiful out of it. Imagination is the key to painting. It's important to me that you're happy. In your world you have total and absolute power.
